My Recipes & Pantry

Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 21, 2026

Overview

My Recipes & Pantry ("the App") is developed by BootheBoys. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data you enter (pantry items, grocery lists, nutrition information) is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account if you choose to enable sync.

Camera Usage

The App uses your device's camera solely to scan barcodes on food products. Images from the camera are processed on-device and are never stored, uploaded, or shared.

Third-Party Services

When you scan a barcode, the App queries the Open Food Facts public database to retrieve product information (name, brand, nutrition). Only the barcode number is sent in this request. No personal data is transmitted.

iCloud

If you enable iCloud sync or Family Sharing, your data is stored in your private iCloud container managed by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data. Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud usage.

Analytics & Tracking

The App does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. We do not track your usage in any way.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
