Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I add items? Tap the + button to add items manually, or use the barcode scanner to add products instantly.
  • Is nutrition data accurate? Nutrition data is sourced from a public database. We recommend verifying values against the product label.
  • Can I share my pantry with family? Yes, go to Settings and use the Family Sharing feature to share your pantry via iCloud.
  • How do I track expiration dates? When adding a pantry item, toggle on "Has expiration date" and select the date. You'll receive notifications before items expire.
